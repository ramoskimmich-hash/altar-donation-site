Our Mission

Serving at the Sacred Altar

Our altar servers are the quiet pillars of every Mass — young men and women who arrive early, prepare the sanctuary, and assist our priests with devoted hearts. They are the hands and feet of our liturgy.

Yet many of our servers lack proper vestments — worn albs, mismatched sizes, and scarce supplies mean they cannot always present themselves with the dignity their calling deserves.

Your donation will purchase quality cassocks, surplices, albs, and accessories for every server in our parish. This is more than clothing — it is an investment in the next generation of faithful servants.
